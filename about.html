About Us

Wecome to Udayaravi Enterprises

Udayaravi Enterprises, incepted in the year 2005, having its facility in Cubbonpet, Bangalore, is primarily into the biz of large scale manufacture and designing of state-of-the-art motor bracking unit, jacquard punching machines and computerized jacquards. The company takes pride in being the first manufacturer of programmed, hi-tech jacquards that facilitate the operation and control of handlooms and powerlooms. The company has received critical acclaim fromCSTRI (Central Silk Technology Research Institute), India, for developing precision, technologically advanced jacquards. A big breakthrough is that the implementation of such design in textile machines received a patent.We have also copyrighted our jacquard brand.

Jacquards from Udayaravi Enterprises fitted to a power loom, simplify the process of textile manufacture with such sophisticated patterns as brocade, damask, and matelassé. These jacquards add control mechanism that automates patterning. A number of punched cards linked together in a series, in tandem with the jacquards, control the looms. The jacquards, manufactured by Udayaravi Enterprises, are extensively used by weavers who fabricate ethnic, traditional sarees, shawls, various kinds of towels and shawls, furnishing fabric, carpets, and mats. The special feature of the jacquards is the individual, middle design repeats, from and to picks. They come with a wide range of hook formats, satisfying various requirements.

We are soon to introduce ROBO TWIN looms that facilitate flexibility, large-scale production, and hand-woven fabric quality. These high-tech handlooms will be the next revolution. We will also start manufacturing the newfangled product, jacquards, integrated with Wi-Fi capability that enables weavers to operate the machine remotely.

Own computerized jacquards and increase the productivity and quality. Be wary while purchasing a jacquard from the market as there are a few fake manufacturers in the textile market.

History

Ravikumar, the creative head and managing director of Udayaravi Enterprises, was born to a farmer, in Kolar district, from whom he inherited the art of growing mulberry leaves and rearing silkworm, to produce conventional silk. His father was also skillful in cocoon cooking, cocoon drying, reeling and twisting of silk, operation of hand punch cards and leg punch cards. Ravi’s father began this business in 1976 and imparted this specially convened skill to him at a very tenderage of 16 years. With years of experience, coupled with hard work and brainpower, he picked up comprehensive know-how on silk and silk production.

He took this business to a whole new level, by venturing into intimately related fields, including silk cocoon reeling, twisting, and warping, setting up a facility at Shidlaghatta, Chikkaballapura district, in Karnataka, in 1992. His burning desire to expand in this industry made him enter into textile designing, in 1998. His inventive skills, later on, made him develop the very machine that can weave sarees, of various designs. In 2005, he began the manufacture of card punching machines, for fabricating different kinds of sarees. His accomplishments didn’t cease here. Being a technology enthusiast, Ravikumar revolutionized the weaving industry by instrumentally introducing the first ever computerized jacquards for hand, power, and rapier looms, to the subcontinent, in 2007. It made life easier for weavers by adding value to the traditional machines.
